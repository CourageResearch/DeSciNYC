DeSciNYC is a New York City-based meetup group that focuses on the intersection of science and blockchain. The group brings together science enthusiasts, decentralization advocates, and anyone interested in exploring innovative research and ideas. Our events attract a diverse and highly intelligent audience, including PhDs and science professionals who are deeply passionate about these topics. Each event draws around 200 attendees and is part networking and part speaker session. Speakers range from professors and graduate students to researchers at the forefront of scientific discovery.

For more information, visit our website at https://desci.nyc.
